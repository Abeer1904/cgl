Chuppi Todo

A card-cum-board game to build understanding, vocabulary, and agency around violence against women and domestic violence.

Theme: Gender Format: Card + Board Players: 5–8 Ages: 14–45

About the Game

Chuppi Todo is a card-cum-board game designed to engage participants and deepen their knowledge on violence against women, particularly domestic violence.

The game includes multiple components linked to DV, including cases, the cycle of violence, types and locations of violence, identifying victim and perpetrator(s), mapping support networks, and accessing referral paths and institutional structures available for survivors.

It encourages discussion on prevalence, builds understanding of legal systems, and supports the agency of women to raise their voice against violence.
