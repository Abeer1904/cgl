Climate Crusaders

A climate spotting, riddle-based board game that builds everyday climate literacy through observation and clues.

Theme: Climate Format: Board Players: 4–8 Includes: 67 clue cards · 1 timer

About the Game

Climate Crusaders helps players spot elements of climate change on a single illustrated board that represents images from everyday life and the impacts of actions on the climate.

Players read clues in riddle form and locate corresponding elements on the board, building recognition, vocabulary, and pattern awareness.

Gameplay and Design

Group vs Group

  • Board in the centre. Teams on either side.
  • Each team reads a clue and finds the answer on the board.
  • 1 minute per turn. Winner is most answers in 5 minutes.

Individual Play

  • Each player gets 30 seconds to solve a clue.
  • Answer cards support deciphering, but you still must find the element.

What It Explores

Signals

Climate impacts and drivers embedded in daily life and infrastructure.

Systems

Energy, industry, consumption, and sustainability strategies on one board.

Learning

Observation, interpretation, and rapid recall through play.
